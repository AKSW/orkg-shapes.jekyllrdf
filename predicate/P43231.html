Excited-state reduction potential

Photoexcited molecules exhibit increased reduction and oxidation potentials compared to their ground states and the resulting excited-state potentials can be estimated, according to the free enthalpy change of a photoinduced electron transfer, by measuring the ground-state potentials and the transition energy.
