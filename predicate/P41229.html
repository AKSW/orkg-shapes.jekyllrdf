Nanophase ferric oxide (nm)

Iron oxide minerals are the prime candidates for Fe(III) signatures in remotely sensed Martian surface spectra. Nanophase ferric oxide (np-Ox)' is a generic term for ferric oxide/oxihydroxide particles having nanoscale (less than 10 nm) particle dimensions
